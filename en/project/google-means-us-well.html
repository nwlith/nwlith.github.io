Google Means Us Well, Editorial Design, 2017.
Project completed with Marion Millier.
14.8 × 21 cm, 16 pages, risography.

Two texts are laid out in confrontation : "Google nous veut du bien", Google means us well, is a 2014 lecture report presenting new Google products. And the second text "Il est venu le temps de la déconnexion", it is time for disconnection, it's a critical manifesto on Google’s influence on our society. We worked on the idea of corrupted digital data, that can reveals and conceals at the same time. Indeed, the conference report offers a double reading, which reveals itself to the attentive reader : the lecture is actually a hoax performed by the German collective Peng!.
This workshop was conducted by Adrien Zammit from the studio Formes Vives.
