The Museum's Greenhouses, Editorial Design, 2016.
42 × 59,4 cm, double-sided printing on 80g paper.

A fictive project combining botanical illustration and micro cartography. A project combining botanical illustration and micro cartography. The choice to print on an almost transparent, low weight paper refers to the fragility of greenhouses' architecture.
