Visual Identity of the Kiffe ta Race Podcast,
Binge Audio, 2019.

"It is in the perception of people that races are built and enter the field of social reality. So much so that interaction between human beings, when it are guided by this idea, is constructed according to these races, however imaginary they may be. Taking into consideration the social perception of races is therefore necessary for the social sciences, for which "races" are effective social categories. It is racism that makes races exist and not the other way round".* — Rokhaya Diallo

In the podcast Kiffe ta Race Rokhaya Diallo and Grace Ly talk about the racism they experience daily and intimately.
