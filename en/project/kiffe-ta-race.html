Visual Identity of the Kiffe ta Race Podcast,
Binge Audio, 2019.

"It is through the perception of people that races are built and enter the field of social reality. To the extent that when human interaction is orientated by this idea, it is built according to these races, however imaginary. Taking into account the social perception of races is therefore necessary for social sciences for which "races" are effective social categories. Racism is what makes races exist, not the other way round."* — Rokhaya Diallo

In the podcast Kiffe ta Race Rokhaya Diallo and Grace Ly talk about the racism they experience daily and intimately.

* Personal translation.
