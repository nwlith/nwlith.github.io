City Traces, illustration, 2016.
40 × 200 cm.

« De l'éclat dans l'ombre des fenêtres,
Des regards assoupis se ferment sous la douceur sombre
des bruits de la ville* » — Aline Louvard

City Traces is a dive into a visual harvest of what constitutes a city. The format, large, is immersive.

*"From the radiance in the windows' shadows, sleepy looks are closing under the dark sweetness of the city's noise"
