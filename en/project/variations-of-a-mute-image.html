Variations of a Mute Image, Master's project, 2019.
40 × 30 cm, 8 formats.

Typing the word « digital » in the image section of a search engine currently results in a range of representations, from an undifferentiated set of blue toned visuals composed of 0 and 1, to electronic circuits and networks of all kinds. The image that fails to make sense is a mute image. It even impedes understanding. This is a research preamble aiming to understand and characterize « digital » and its mute representation.
